  <h4>Tips</h4>
  
  <h5>Reopen with Maximized Browser</h5>
  <p>To open the browser with a maximized window, use "--start-maximized" as a parameter.</p>
  
  <pre><code>
  $ nano /home/user/Desktop/browser.desktop
  </code></pre>
  
  <pre><code>
  [Desktop Entry]
  Exec=/usr/bin/browser --start-maximized %U
  </code></pre>
