Comparative Table of Academic, Business, and NGO Publications

Type Purpose Audience Structure Review Process Examples
Blog Post Share informal insights, opinions, or updates General public, followers Title, Body, Images/Media, References Not peer-reviewed, may be edited Company blogs, personal blogs
Newsletter Provide regular updates and information to subscribers Subscribers, members, stakeholders Title, Introduction, Articles/Sections, Links Internally reviewed Company newsletters, NGO newsletters
Press Release Announce news or updates to the media Journalists, media outlets Title, Date, Body, Contact Information Internally reviewed Corporate press releases, NGO press releases
Brochure Provide information or promote products/services Potential customers, general public Title, Body, Images, Contact Information Internally reviewed Product brochures, NGO program brochures
Business Case Study Provide a detailed analysis of a real-world business situation Business professionals, clients, students Introduction, Background, Case Presentation, Analysis, Conclusion Internally reviewed or reviewed by business peers Harvard Business Review case studies, industry case studies
Report Provide detailed information and analysis on a specific topic or project Project stakeholders, management, clients, regulatory bodies Title Page, Executive Summary, Introduction, Methodology, Findings, Analysis, Conclusion, Recommendations, Appendices Internally reviewed Annual reports, research reports, project reports
Manual/Guide Provide instructions and information on the use of a product or system End users, technicians, engineers Title Page, Table of Contents, Introduction, Instructions, Troubleshooting, Appendices Internally reviewed, sometimes user-tested Software manuals, machinery manuals
Policy Brief Summarize information to inform policy decisions Policymakers, government officials, stakeholders Title, Executive Summary, Introduction, Analysis, Recommendations Internally reviewed, sometimes peer-reviewed Government policy briefs, NGO policy briefs
White Paper Inform and guide decision-making on complex issues Government officials, business stakeholders, industry professionals Title Page, Executive Summary, Introduction, Problem Statement, Background, Solution, Benefits, Conclusion, References Usually internally reviewed, not always peer-reviewed Government policy documents, industry technology guides
Essay Present arguments, analyses, or interpretations on a topic Students, educators Introduction, Body, Conclusion, References Reviewed by instructors College essays, argumentative essays
Academic Case Study Detailed analysis of a particular case within a real-world context Academics, students, researchers Introduction, Background, Case Presentation, Analysis, Conclusion Peer-reviewed Clinical case studies, educational case studies
Paper Broad term for any scholarly writing including articles, conference papers, etc. Academics, students, researchers Variable depending on type (e.g., term paper, working paper, conference paper) May or may not be peer-reviewed Conference papers, term papers, working papers
Article Present original research, review existing research, or offer theoretical insights Academics, researchers, professionals Title, Abstract, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, References Peer-reviewed Journal of Applied Physics, Nature, Science
Thesis/Dissertation Present the research and findings of a student's graduate-level work Academics, examiners, researchers Title Page, Abstract, Introduction, Literature Review, Methodology, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, References Reviewed by a committee, often includes a defense PhD dissertations, Master's theses
